Step 1

Set your projection and tax impact

This calculator is designed to show how much cash remains in your bank account after your normal yearly income, recurring monthly expenses, and the tax amount you choose to include manually.

Step 2

Add your annual income sources

Add an income and press SAVE. Each saved item will appear below. Repeat to add more.

Step 3

Add your monthly expenses

Add an expense and press SAVE. Each saved item will appear below. Repeat to add more. You can optionally set annual increase.

Step 4

Your savings results

These results show what remains in your bank account after expenses, with and without your manual annual tax adjustment.
